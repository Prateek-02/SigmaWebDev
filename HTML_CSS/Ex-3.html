Manali

Manali, nestled in the picturesque state of Himachal Pradesh in India, is a captivating hill station renowned for its breathtaking natural beauty, serene landscapes, and adventurous activities. Situated at an altitude of 2,050 meters (6,726 feet) above sea level, Manali offers a perfect retreat for travelers seeking solace amidst the mighty Himalayas.
